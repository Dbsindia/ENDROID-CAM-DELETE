Account Deletion Request

Endroid Cam - Camera Management App
Last updated: 27-02-2026

DBS SECURITY SYSTEMS PVT. LTD. operates the Endroid Cam mobile application. This page explains how users can request deletion of their Endroid Cam account and associated personal data.

How to Delete Your Endroid Cam Account

You can delete your Endroid Cam account using one of the following methods:

Option 1: Delete Account Within the App

  • Open the Endroid Cam app
  • Log in using your registered email
  • Open Profile Screen
  • Scroll down to bottom of the screen
  • Click on Delete Account
  • Confirm the deletion

Option 2: Request Account Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting us via email:

  • Email: naman@dbsindia.in
  • Subject: Endroid Cam Account Deletion Request
  • Please include your registered email address

What Data Is Deleted

Once your account deletion request is processed, the following data is permanently deleted:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Account credentials
  • All associated camera configurations are deleted
  • User-related configuration data

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • Your personal data is permanently removed from our systems
Important:
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, you will no longer be able to access your account.
